Privacy Policy for DeBoard Now

Last updated: November 28, 2025

DeBoard Now ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Data

DeBoard Now collects and processes your device's location information to provide core functionality:

  • Foreground Location: Used when the app is open to calculate distance to your destination.
  • Background Location: Used when the app is closed or your phone is locked to continue tracking your journey and trigger alarms when you approach your destination.

Important: Your location data is processed entirely on your device. We do NOT store, transmit, or share your location history with any servers or third parties.

1.2 Device Information

We may collect basic device information including:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers (for analytics only)

1.3 Advertising Data (AdMob)

DeBoard Now uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (for personalized ads)
  • Approximate location (city/region level)
  • App usage patterns
  • Device information (screen size, OS version)

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Calculate distance between your current location and destination
  • Trigger alarms and notifications at appropriate distances (2km and 500m)
  • Display zone-based progress indicators
  • Show relevant advertisements via Google AdMob
  • Improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal location data with third parties, except:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements (as described in Section 1.3)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulation

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Location tracking happens locally on your device
  • No location history is stored on external servers
  • Journey data is temporary and deleted when you end a trip
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke Location Permissions: Disable location access in your device settings at any time (this will stop the app from functioning)
  • Opt-out of Personalized Ads: Disable personalized advertising in your Google Account settings or device ad settings
  • Delete Data: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

6. Children's Privacy

DeBoard Now is intended for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. The app does not include features specifically designed for children.

7. Third-Party Services

DeBoard Now integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob Privacy Policy
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim: For place search functionality (no personal data shared)

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be notified through:

  • App update notifications
  • Updated "Last Modified" date on this page

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. International Users

DeBoard Now is designed for global use. Location processing happens on your device regardless of your location. By using the app, you consent to the processing of your data as described in this policy.
